SPEN Digital Archive

The digital repository of the Sociedade Portuguesa de Entomologia provides open access to bulletins, proceedings, catalogues and scientific articles relevant to Portuguese and Iberian entomology.

About

Sociedade Portuguesa de Entomologia is a scientific society dedicated to the study of insects and other arthropods, supporting taxonomy, ecology, conservation and applied entomology in Portugal and related regions.

This digital archive preserves and disseminates publications issued or curated by the Sociedade Portuguesa de Entomologia, including the Boletim da Sociedade Portuguesa de Entomologia, thematic supplements, conference proceedings and specialist catalogues.

The collection includes peer-reviewed articles, faunistic records, checklists, historical documentation and institutional material, all organized for search, filtering and direct citation.
